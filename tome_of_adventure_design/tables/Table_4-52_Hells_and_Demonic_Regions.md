# Table 4-52: Hells and Demonic Regions

```
Table 4-52: Hells and Demonic Regions
Die Roll

Varied Factor in the Hell (d100)
01-20
Altered form: “visitors” may manifest with demonic physical attributes – or possibly appear angelic, since their
morality is so alien to the plane
21-40
Despair, greed, rage, or other mental state creeps up on characters if they fail periodic saving throws
41-60
Fire or cold spells do not function properly, or are more powerful than normal
61-80
Temperature: temperatures, whether hot or cold, are so extreme as to be dangerous
81-00
Wounds regenerate, but re-grown flesh is monstrous or demonic
```
